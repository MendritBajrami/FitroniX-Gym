CALCULATE YOUR BMI

A Fitness BMI Calculator is a tool that helps you estimate your Body Mass Index (BMI) based on your weight and height. It provides a quick assessment of whether you're underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese according to standardized health ranges. While it doesn't measure body fat directly, it's commonly used to gauge general fitness and health risk levels.
